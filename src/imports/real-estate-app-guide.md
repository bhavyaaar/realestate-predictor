5. EXACT Prompt You Can Paste Into Figma AI

Copy this:

Design a modern real estate analytics dashboard web app called “HomeScope”. The layout should include a top navigation bar with a search bar and navigation links (Buy, Rent, Market Trends, Saved Homes). On the left side include a filter sidebar with price range, bedrooms, bathrooms, property type, school rating, commute time, and climate risk filters. In the center show an interactive map with property pins. On the right show a property detail panel with images, price, beds/baths, square footage, and a price prediction chart. Below the map include a market insights section with analytics cards showing median price trends, population growth, and investment score. Use a clean modern SaaS design style with blue (#1E3A8A), sky blue (#3B82F6), emerald accent (#10B981), and light gray background (#F8FAFC). Use rounded cards, subtle shadows, and modern typography similar to Zillow or Redfin.
